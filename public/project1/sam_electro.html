About Sam Electronics


Sam Electronics is a start of the art repair shop for all things electronic. It's been in the Missisauga area for about a year now; however, our Tech specialists have been in the business for over 20 years; thus, you can trust any of your devices with us. We also have your devices on site for sale. We're committed to serving the Missisauga area as well as the metroplitan Toronto area. Welcome to our page, scroll down to learn more.
