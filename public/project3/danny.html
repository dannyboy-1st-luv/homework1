So far I've been to all but two continents; I've grew up in Africa, immigrated to North America, visited South America, traveled through Europe and waiting to visit Asia and Australia. I've put together some of the pictures I took from some of these trips. Most of them are also pieces of art from my perspective. So hope you enjoy them, and feel free to leave behind a comment or two. One of the reason travelling is expensive is because each trip teaches you a lesson. The Bible says wisdom cries out in the street. So each time you travel outside your home city you have numerous opportunities to encounter wisdom! But travelling doesn't always have to be expensive. With ample planning you can travel for little to nothing. There are a lot of great deals on groupon for packaged trips and experiences all around the world. And you don't have to stay in hotels; airbnb is a perfect pick and it's everywhere! And you don't always have to rent a car. Using local transportation helps you to be submerged into the culture and experience life in the city or country you find yourself in. In Central America for instance, there are buses that go all through the region. Finally travel is important, because as believers we're supposed to go into ALL the world and preach the gospel to every creature. How can we succesfully do that if we're not used to travelling, and only used to sitting at home? Anyway, all this blabbering was to help fill the page so I can present this to my teacher hehehe. Above all, I hope you enjoy the pictures. I still have a lot to learn when it comes to programming. So Lord help me!
